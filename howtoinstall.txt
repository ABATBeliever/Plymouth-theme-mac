<Auto install>
1: 
cd plymouth-theme-mac-black
or
cd plymouth-theme-mac-white

2:
chmod +x install-mac-black-theme.sh
or
chmod +x install-mac-white-theme.sh

3:
sudo ./install-mac-black-theme.sh
or
sudo ./install-mac-white-theme.sh

==========================================================
<Manual install>
1:
copy to /usr/share/plymouth/themes/mac-black/
or
copy to /usr/share/plymouth/themes/mac-white/

2:
sudo update-alternatives --install /usr/share/plymouth/themes/default.plymouth default.plymouth /usr/share/plymouth/themes/mac-black/mac-black.plymouth 100
or
sudo update-alternatives --install /usr/share/plymouth/themes/default.plymouth default.plymouth /usr/share/plymouth/themes/mac-white/mac-white.plymouth 100

3:
sudo update-alternatives --config default.plymouth

4:
sudo update-initramfs -u
sudo reboot
